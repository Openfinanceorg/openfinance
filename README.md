# OpenFinance App

Connect your bank accounts to your own AI. Self-hosted and open source—you own your data and feed your finances into the AI tools you choose.

OpenFinance links your accounts (via open banking), stores transactions in your own database, and exposes them through a simple API and UI so you can use them with any AI assistant or workflow.

**Stack:** Hono, SvelteKit, Drizzle ORM, Better Auth, PostgreSQL, Docker
